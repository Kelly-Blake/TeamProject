How to Play

Click the video to learn how to play.

Instructions:

Step 1: Click the start button to begin the game. You will start at Level 1. To begin click the yellow circle with the number 1. Next click the squares with the number 1 until you have colored all the boxes with 1. To continue click the white circle labeled 2. Color in all the squares with number 2. Repeat the steps for number 3. Once you have finished coloring the page and reveal the image click the next button.
Step 2: You are now on Level 2. Repeat the previous steps to finish the picture in Level 2. After you finish painting Level 2 click the next button and move on to level 3.
Step 3: Level 3 is the final level. Follow the previous steps to color the final image.
